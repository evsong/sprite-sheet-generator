## Legal Pages

### Requirements

#### REQ-1: Privacy Policy Page
The site must have a `/privacy` page accessible to all visitors.

**Scenarios:**
- User navigates to `/privacy` → sees complete privacy policy covering: data collection, usage, storage, third-party sharing, cookies, user rights, contact info
- Footer contains link to `/privacy`

#### REQ-2: Terms of Service Page
The site must have a `/terms` page accessible to all visitors.

**Scenarios:**
- User navigates to `/terms` → sees complete terms covering: service description, user obligations, intellectual property, limitation of liability, refund policy, termination, governing law
- Footer contains link to `/terms`
